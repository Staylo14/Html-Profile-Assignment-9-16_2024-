About Lóren

More than the sum of his parts, Lóren, per the root of his name, has high integrity, strong character and a humble but sesible belief in his abilites as a creator. Lóren is... a description defying nonconformist with a mind and hear open to not only exploreing what is possible but creating it.

Compsotional Process

Lóren's compositional process varies from project to project, however as of late he seems to enjoy starting at the piano. At the piano, Lóren will either write a melody or come up with an ear captivating chord progression. From then on, Lóren will build the song by adding what he thinks the song needs.
